Valtari

Valtari is the sixth full-length studio album by the Icelandic band Sigur Rós, released on May 23, 2012 in Japan, and May 28 and 29 in most other territories.

The album was announced in an interview with Q Magazine on March 26, 2012. Bassist Georg Hólm described the album as having "more electronic stuff than before" but not being "a dance album". The first single, "Ekki Múkk", as well as an accompanying video, was released on the same day.
